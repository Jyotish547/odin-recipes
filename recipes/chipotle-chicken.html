How to make Creamy Chipotle Chicken

A delicious slow-cooker recipe for creamy chipotle chicken with a slight kick! Serve chicken with rice and spoon some sauce over the top.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Â½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, cut into cubes
  • 3 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce from chipotle peppers

Directions

  1. Place chicken breasts into a slow cooker, brush with butter, and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and minced garlic. Pour in condensed soup and chicken broth. Add cream cheese, chipotle peppers, and adobo sauce. Stir the mixture gently.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours.
